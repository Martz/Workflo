Reports


The Reports Menu provides in excess of 40 reports enabling you to print or just view every aspect of your fleets refuelling operation. To run a report, mark your selected report by clicking on it in the list and then click the Run Report button.Depending on the Report selected you will be asked to enter and confirm the data range that the Report should cover. This will generally always include a date range.To see a short description of the detail provided by each report, mark the report and click Brief Report Description.
